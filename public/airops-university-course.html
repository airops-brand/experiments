What makes this different

Not a video library.

Every section is built to make you do something, not just watch something. Three formats keep you engaged throughout.

Step-by-step screen recordings that walk you through real product flows. Pause, follow along, and build as you learn.

Embedded exercises

Each section includes hands-on tasks inside your own AirOps workspace. Theory turns into practice before you move on.

Click-through modules

Interactive decision trees and guided prompts that teach you to think through AEO strategy, not just execute steps.
